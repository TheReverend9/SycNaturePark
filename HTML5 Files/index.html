Welcome to Syc Nature Park, LLC

Syc Nature Story

Welcome to Syc Nature Park, LLC where we broaden our campers vision! We are a new and upcoming RV Park where our mission is to set ourselves apart from any other campground! We are a family owned and operated park located on the Lauren’s side of Lake Greenwood. Amidst 21 acres of breathtakingly beautiful property there is an adventure awaiting to unfold for anyone! We are a small private park with phenomenal vision!

What we offer at Syc Nature

Mountain Mans Trail

Get in touch with nature by taking a walk down our 1/2 mile picturesque walking trail thru the forest while capturing photos of the wild in the their natural habitats or picking wild muscadines! You’ll cross bridges along the path that leads to a charming island inserted between the creek beds where a second firepit is located in the center of the island! Cross a bridge and a swing is tucked along the creek side for relaxing! Cross another bridge to access our grill area and picnic table for creek side dinner! The island is a perfect place to relax and enjoy the evening!

Dollie's Catch and Release

Enjoy the 2 acre catch and release fish pond located in the center of the property! Catch beautiful sunsets from the dock or hop in to cool off from the heat! The water is 12 ft deep at the dock so no worries about touching the bottom for those of us who don’t like to touch!

Eugene's Pyro Pits

Enjoy one of our unique firepits located throughout the park! Lots of memories to be made around these bonfires!

Grandma Blanche's Fish Station

We offer a fish cleaning station for those of you coming off the lake! We also offer fish cleaning services as well! Educated in fish cleaning and filetin by Newberrys finest fisherman Mrs. Blanche Cromer, known to everyone as Grandma Blanche!

About the Owner

The story behind the development of Syc Nature

My name is Melissa Blackmon owner, designer, and developer of Syc Nature Park, LLC. After raising four kids on my own, I began chasing a dream of owning a campground in 2018! I made that dream a reality in 2022 along with the help of my hard working children and daughter n laws! Come watch the desire and passion behind the hearts that build and grow this park!
